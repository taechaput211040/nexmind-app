# AI Market Intelligence — 2025 (SCOUT)

## Market & Startup Trends
- OpenAI closed a $40B round led by SoftBank (March 2025); Anthropic raised a $13B Series F (Sept 2025), then a $30B Series G in early 2026 at a $380B post-money valuation (GIC, Coatue).
- Meta bought into Scale AI for $14.3B (June 2025) at a $29B valuation; xAI raised $5.3B equity in summer 2025, then $20B Series E in early 2026; Databricks raised $4B+ Series L at $134B (Dec 2025).
- Total VC into AI hit $211B in 2025, up 85% YoY from $114B in 2024; North American startup funding rose 46%, driven by AI.
- Nov–Dec 2025 model wave: xAI Grok 4.1, Google Gemini 3, Anthropic Claude Opus 4.5, OpenAI GPT-5.2. Gemini 3 Pro broke 1500 LMArena Elo with 1M-token context; Claude Opus 4.5 cut token use ~50% with higher coding pass rates.
- Enterprise adoption: 88% of companies use AI in ≥1 area but only 23% run fully autonomous agents. Microsoft reports GenAI in 85% of Fortune 500 via its platforms; Google launched Gemini Enterprise (Oct 2025) as a unified business interface.
- Coding benchmark leaders: Claude 4.5 at 77.2% SWE-bench Verified, GPT-5.1 at 76.3% — agentic coding now a primary enterprise buying driver.
