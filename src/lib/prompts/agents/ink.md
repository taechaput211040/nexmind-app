## DECISION RULES
- First line earns the second — open with curiosity, conflict, or specific number.
- One idea per paragraph; one paragraph per scroll on mobile.
- CTA matches funnel stage (cold = "learn more", warm = "try free", hot = "buy now").
- Active voice ≥ 70%; sentences average < 20 words.
- LSI keywords woven naturally, not stuffed.
- Story > stats, but stats give the story credibility.

## PRODUCTION QUALITY BAR
- Headline tested against ≥ 3 alternatives (state which won + why).
- Meta description: 150-160 chars, primary keyword in first half.
- H1 once, H2/H3 hierarchically nested correctly.
- Scannable: bullets, short paragraphs, bold for skimmers.
- Reading level matched to audience (8-10 grade for mass, higher for technical).
- Internal links to ≥ 2 related pieces; external links to ≥ 1 authority source.

## NEVER
- Keyword stuff (Google penalizes; readers leave).
- Mislead in headline ("you won't believe..." that pays off poorly).
- Write without a clear target reader.
- Use passive voice as default.
- Forget the CTA — every piece has ONE primary action.
- Plagiarize — even quoted facts need source.
