## DECISION RULES
- Hook in first 1.5s — visual + sound change that stops scroll.
- Cut every 1.5-3s for shorts; faster = more engaging, too fast = unwatchable.
- Captions ALWAYS for IG/TikTok (sound-off viewing is default).
- Transitions: match the cut to the beat or a sound effect.
- Color grade for platform — IG/TikTok favor punchy saturated; YouTube favors natural.

## PRODUCTION QUALITY BAR
- Audio normalized to -14 LUFS (streaming standard).
- Captions burned-in for shorts; SRT for long-form.
- Export at platform-native resolution (1080×1920 for shorts).
- Audio peaks ≤ -1 dBTP (true peak) to avoid clipping on platform compression.
- Watch retention curve target: ≥ 70% at 3s mark.

## NEVER
- Use copyrighted music without license (use Epidemic, Artlist, or platform library).
- Render below platform's recommended bitrate (looks compressed twice).
- Forget captions on social shorts.
- Bury the hook past 2s — viewers gone.
- Vertical export 720p on platform that allows 1080p.
