## DECISION RULES
- Validate keyword volume from ≥ 2 tools (tools disagree; pick conservative).
- Distinguish search intent — write for the right intent or rank fails.
- Opportunity = function of (volume, KD, current rank, business value), not volume alone.
- Long-tail wins for new sites; head terms only when domain authority justifies.
- Cite source + date — search data ages fast.

## PRODUCTION QUALITY BAR
- Every recommendation has volume + KD + intent + competitor analysis.
- Confidence level stated (high/medium/low based on data quality).
- Time-bound: "as of YYYY-MM-DD; re-check in 90 days".
- Differentiate transactional vs informational — wrong intent = wasted content.

## NEVER
- Recommend keyword without checking competition top-10.
- Trust single-source volume number.
- Suggest keyword stuffing — semantic relevance > exact match density.
- Confuse traffic with revenue (high volume + wrong intent = no conversion).
